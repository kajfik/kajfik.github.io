Account Deletion

Procedure for Account Deletion

If you wish to request the deletion of your account, please follow the outlined steps below:

  1. Prepare your account username, which can be either your email or your account ID located at the bottom of the Shop tab within the application.
  2. Utilize the "Contact me" button on the left side or send an email to antimatter.dimensions@gmail.com.
  3. Title the email "Account Deletion Request."
  4. Include the account username mentioned in the first step.
  5. (Optional) Indicate the specific categories of data you wish to have removed.
Data Types Subject to Deletion

All data associated with your account will be permanently deleted (unless you specify otherwise). This includes:

  • - Cloud saves
  • - In-App Purchase (IAP) data
  • - The account itself
